The Exploration of Biodiesel

What are we doing?

We are creating and testing for the effectiveness of biodiesel as it is a sustainable fuel source. This is done to see which types of biodiesel is the most efficient while also raising awareness for the sustainability goals number 7 and 11.

The benefits of biodiesel

When consumed, biodiesel will only emit a small amount of carbon dioxide, releasing less greenhouse gasses into the atmosphere compared to its fossil fuel counterpart.
Not only that, research shows that there will be less toxic air pollutants released from vehicles if they use biodiesel instead of fossil fuels.

How will we create and measure the effectiveness of different biodiesels?

In summary, we used different types of oils and added some potassium hydroxide and methanol to them to create biodiesel.
We then measured the effectiveness of each biodiesel via measuring the temperature change when heating water with the fuel. Higher the temperature, the more efficient the biodiesel is.
